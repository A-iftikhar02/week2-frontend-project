About Travelly

Travelly is your go-to platform for curated travel guides, destination tips, and inspirational itineraries. We aim to help travelers plan stress-free adventures packed with memorable experiences.

About the Author

Abdullah Iftikhar is a passionate traveler and storyteller who has explored over 30 countries. With an eye for detail and a love for discovering hidden gems, they created Travelly to share practical advice, cultural insights, and stunning destinations with fellow explorers.

When not traveling, Abdullah Iftikhar enjoys photography, hiking, and trying new cuisines. Through Travelly, they hope to inspire others to embark on unforgettable journeys around the globe.

Mission & Vision

Our mission is to make travel planning simple, enjoyable, and inspiring for everyone. Our vision is a connected world where people explore, respect, and appreciate different cultures.
